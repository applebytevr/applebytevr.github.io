UNDERSTANDING.

WHAT DATA DO WE COLLECT?

For moderating/security reasons, we collect the following:

IP Addresses

PlayFab ID

Oculus ID

Device ID

HOW DO WE USE THIS DATA?

Applebyte uses this data for either moderating users, tracking score on things such as Oculus Leaderboards, and preventing users from ban evading. IP Addresses are stored inside of PlayFab, and are used to prevent users from creating another account and ban evading.

HOW WE CAN REMOVE SAID DATA.

To remove collected data, you can contact us at our support email located at the Contact tab, or at support@applebytevr.com. You can also contact us on our Discord server, also located in the Contact tab.
